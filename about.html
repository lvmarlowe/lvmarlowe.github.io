Madame Fluffycorn


the psychic


Madame Fluffycorn is a psychic extraordinaire who stumbled upon her gift during a particularly intense game of hide-and-seek. While hiding in a broom closet, she suddenly became aware of the exact location of all the other players, even though they were scattered throughout the house. From that moment on, she knew she had a special talent for sensing the unseen.

When she's not practicing her mystical craft, Madame Fluffycorn loves to indulge in her hobbies. She enjoys knitting cozy sweaters for her cats, playing the ukulele, and collecting unicorn figurines. Her love of unicorns is unbridled, and she channels their magic through her colorful wardrobe and her mystical readings. Her clients often leave her sessions feeling uplifted and inspired to follow their passions, just like Madame Fluffycorn's love for all things magical.

MJ Dreyfuss


the artist


MJ Dreyfuss is a professional artist with a penchant for the peculiar and a love for the ludicrous. She began her artistic journey in high school where she was often found squatting between vending machines, drawing everything from unicorns to zombies.

It was during one of these drawing sessions that she was discovered by Madame Fluffycorn, who was trying to dislodge a bag of hot Cheetos from the vending machine. Madame Fluffycorn was immediately captivated by MJ's imaginative doodles. The two quickly bonded over their shared love of the bizarre and the supernatural, and the rest is Oddstrology.

Duncan


the office dog


Duncan is the official office dog of Oddstrology and the loyal companion of Madame Fluffycorn. He may not know the difference between astrology and astronaut, but he makes up for it with his adorably derpy smile and a heart of gold.

Duncan is always ready to lend a paw, whether it's holding down important papers or keeping Madame Fluffycorn's feet warm during late-night stargazing sessions. His superpower is his ability to drool on command, which he uses to remind everyone to stay hydrated. With his floppy ears and wiggly tail, Duncan is a constant source of joy and laughter for the Oddstrology team. When he's not snoozing in his dog bed, he can be found playing fetch or practicing his downward dog pose during office yoga sessions.
